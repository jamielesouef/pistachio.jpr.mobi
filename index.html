Pistachio - Personal Tax Calculator

Pistachio

Pistachio is a simple little app to easily calculate your income in Australia. Going into a pay negotiation? Know how much you want in your pocket per week? Simply enter it into the app and slam that puppy down on your boss's table shouting 'Show me the money' (It's probably best you don't do this, your boss may not see the funny side).

See everything

Pistachio calculates your Annual income, but can also show you your weekly, fortnightly and monthly breakdowns.

Pistachio breaks down your income into the following:

  • Gross Income
  • Taxable Income
  • Net Income
  • Superannuation
  • Income Tax
  • Medicare Levy
  • Other Taxes
  • Low Income Tax Offset
  • Total Taxes

Only the start

Coming soon you will be able to compare different incomes, split your income between two people to work out how to maximise your income, customise the superannuation settings, compare different tax years and more awesome sauce that I've yet to think up.

What are you doing?

Why are you still reading this? Get on the app and see how much money you are giving to the Government...

Whilst care has been taken with the calculations, this app should only be used as an indication of salary only. Seek advice from an accountant for 100% accurate numbers.
